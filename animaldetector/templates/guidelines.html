Wildlife Conflict in Coimbatore

Coimbatore, situated near the Western Ghats, experiences frequent human-wildlife interactions. Here are the most common wildlife encounters in different areas:

Elephants

Periyanaickenpalayam, Anaikatti, Madukkarai, Thadagam, Boluvampatti, Sirumugai

Leopards

Thondamuthur, Karamadai, Mettupalayam, Anaikatti, Narasipuram

Monkeys

Marudhamalai, Perur, Isha, Vellingiri Hills, Vadavalli

Wild Boars

Siruvani, Thondamuthur, Kanuvai, Mettupalayam, Sirumugai

Elephant Encounters

  • Maintain minimum 100 meters distance
  • Avoid honking or using bright lights at night
  • Run in zigzag pattern if charged
  • Use eco-friendly deterrents (chili ropes, bee fences)

Leopard Encounters

  • Stay still and back away slowly
  • Secure livestock in enclosed spaces at night
  • Avoid walking alone after dark in foothill areas
  • Contact TNFD if leopard enters village

Monkey Management

  • Never feed monkeys in temple areas
  • Keep windows closed in prone areas
  • Use secure dustbins for food waste
  • Stay calm if confronted

Wild Boar Safety

  • Don't approach or chase aggressively
  • Climb to higher ground if attacked
  • Use solar-powered electric fences for crops
  • Keep distance and avoid confrontation

Regional First-Aid Guidelines

Elephant-Related Injuries
  • Move victim to safety immediately
  • Control bleeding with clean bandage
  • Rush to Coimbatore Govt Hospital
Snake Bites (Anaikatti Area)
  • Immobilize affected limb
  • Don't attempt venom extraction
  • Contact Snake Rescue Team
Leopard/Boar Attacks
  • Clean wounds with antiseptic
  • Get rabies vaccination
  • Visit nearest medical center
